Frequently Asked Questions

What research methods do you use?

I'm proficient in both qualitative and quantitative research methods including surveys, interviews, case studies, experimental design, and statistical analysis.

Can you help with statistical analysis?

Yes, I provide statistical analysis using SPSS, R, Python, Excel, and other tools. I help with data cleaning, analysis, interpretation, and visualization.

How do you ensure research quality?

I follow rigorous research standards including proper methodology, valid data collection techniques, appropriate statistical tests, and peer-review quality analysis.

Can you help with publication?

Yes, I provide assistance with academic publication including journal selection, manuscript preparation, formatting according to journal guidelines, and response to reviewers.

What if I need primary data collection?

I can help design surveys, questionnaires, and interview guides. For actual data collection, I provide guidance while you conduct the collection to ensure authenticity.

Do you work with specific research frameworks?

Yes, I'm familiar with various research frameworks and theories across disciplines. I adapt the methodology to your specific research questions and theoretical framework.
