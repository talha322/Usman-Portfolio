Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between thesis and dissertation?

A thesis is typically for master's degrees and focuses on existing research, while a dissertation is for doctoral degrees and requires original research contribution.

How long does it take to complete a thesis?

Timeline varies based on complexity and requirements. Typically 4-8 weeks for master's thesis and 8-16 weeks for doctoral dissertation, but custom timelines are available.

Do you help with data collection?

I can guide you on data collection methods and help design surveys/questionnaires, but actual data collection is typically done by the student to ensure authenticity.

Can you help with thesis defense preparation?

Yes, I provide defense preparation including presentation slides, anticipated questions, and talking points to help you confidently defend your thesis.

What if my supervisor requests changes?

I work closely with you to incorporate your supervisor's feedback. Revisions are included in the package to ensure the work meets approval.

Is this service confidential?

Absolutely. Your personal information and thesis details are kept strictly confidential. I also sign NDAs when required.
