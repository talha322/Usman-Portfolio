Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between proofreading and editing?

Proofreading focuses on correcting grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors. Editing includes proofreading plus improving clarity, flow, style, and overall structure of the document.

How long does editing take?

Turnaround time depends on document length and complexity. Standard documents (3000 words) typically take 2-3 days. Rush options are available for urgent deadlines.

What citation styles do you handle?

I'm proficient in all major citation styles including APA, MLA, Harvard, Chicago, IEEE, Vancouver, and more. I follow your specific style guide requirements.

Do you check for plagiarism?

Yes, premium packages include comprehensive plagiarism checks using advanced software. I provide a detailed plagiarism report with identified issues and corrections.

Can you edit non-native English writing?

Absolutely. I specialize in editing work from non-native English speakers, improving grammar, vocabulary, and overall clarity while maintaining your original voice and meaning.

Is my document kept confidential?

Yes, complete confidentiality is guaranteed. Your documents are never shared with third parties and are securely deleted after the editing process is complete.
